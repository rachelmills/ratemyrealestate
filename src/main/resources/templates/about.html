According to the Australian Bureau of Statistic (ABS), almost one-third of households in Australia rent their homes. As a long-term renter in the Brisbane area I have experienced some very good service and some very poor service from rental agencies.

An important part of the rental process is filling out the application form. Prospective tenants are required to declare a huge amount of personal information, including work details, wage slips, personal references, work references and previous addresses. Whilst there may be a valid reason for having to divulge such a large amount of personal information, it is very much a one-way process. The prospective tenant will be able to learn very little about the landlord/landlady and/or the rental agency.

The purpose of this website is to help prospective tenants make more informed choices by sharing feedback on their own experiences with rental agencies.
